History of Techcon

TechCon began in 2010 as a modest gathering of local technology enthusiasts. Since then, it has expanded into a premier international conference, drawing innovators, industry leaders, and visionaries from across the globe. Over the years, TechCon has continuously evolved to highlight groundbreaking technologies, promote knowledge sharing, and foster meaningful collaboration across diverse sectors of the tech industry.

Mission

TechCon SoCal is dedicated to inspiring, educating, and connecting the next generation of tech leaders. Our mission is rooted in a commitment to diversity, innovation, and positive impact—empowering individuals and communities through technology. By fostering an inclusive environment and showcasing forward-thinking ideas, TechCon SoCal drives progress and shapes the future of the tech industry.

Past Speakers

Steve Jobs

Biography

Steve Jobs (1955–2011) was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and a visionary leader in technology. He revolutionized personal computing, digital music, smartphones, and animation with innovations like the Mac, iPod, iPhone, and Pixar films. His work reshaped how the world interacts with technology.

Contribution

Co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and introduced the Apple II and Macintosh, which made computers more accessible and user-friendly through graphical interfaces.

Susanne Tarkowski Tempelhof

Biography

Susanne Tarkowski Tempelhof is a Swedish-French entrepreneur, writer, and founder of Bitnation, the world’s first blockchain-based virtual nation, launched in 2014. Her work focuses on creating decentralized, voluntary governance systems that operate independently of traditional nation-states.

Contribution

The inventor of bitnation a system of documentation using the blockchain
